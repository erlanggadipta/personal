Our Recommendation

Main Insight:
Based on pizza sales data from one year in 2015,
we can see that the Classic category has the highest sales, followed by
the Supreme, Chicken, and Veggie categories in that order.

Growth Potential:
Since the Classic category is the most popular,
there is a significant opportunity to further increase revenue by
leveraging this popularity. One strategy that can be adopted
is to introduce new variations in the Classic category or enhance
promotions to boost sales of products in this category.

Optimizing the Supreme Category:
Although the Supreme category ranks second
in sales, there is potential to further optimize sales in
this category. This can be achieved by identifying customer preferences
and adjusting marketing strategies and product offerings to increase
the appeal of the Supreme category.

Menu Diversification:
Seeing that the Chicken and Veggie categories are in lower
positions in sales, there are opportunities to enhance menu variety in
these categories. There might be unmet demand from certain market segments
that can be explored to attract more customers.

Bundling Offers:
Given customer preferences seen from sales data,
bundling offer strategies can be applied. For instance, offering packages
that include products from both the Classic and Supreme categories at attractive prices can
drive combined sales and increase revenue.

Seasonal Analysis:
Additionally, it's important to analyze sales patterns
based on seasons or specific periods. For example, there might be an increase
in sales during holiday seasons or weekends that can be leveraged by
adjusting promotional strategies.
